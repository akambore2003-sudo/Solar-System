Kim Taehyung

Kim Taehyung (known professionally as V) is a South Korean singer, songwriter, and actor.

Born on December 30, 1995, he rose to global superstardom as a lead dancer, sub-vocalist, and visual for the acclaimed boy band BTS.

Signature Style He is celebrated for his deep, soulful baritone vocals and charismatic stage presence.

Solo Success Alongside his work with BTS, he has released hit solo tracks like "Singularity" and "Stigma",

as well as acclaimed solo projects like his debut album Layover
